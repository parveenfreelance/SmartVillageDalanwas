Village History

Dalanwas was established in Samvat 1454 by Daluram after migration from Pitampura (Delhi) to Chandaliya.

Spiritual Heritage

Saint Savantia became Shri Shri 1008 Siddh Shri Baba Sawanth Nath. Temples exist in Salas, Bhirod, and Pralnwas.

Social Harmony

Jats, Brahmins, Sunars, Kumhars, Jogis, Dhanaks, Chamars live together peacefully.

Education

Facilities include GGS School, Library, Sports Ground, Fawara Chowk and Pidhiya Dham.

Pride & Achievements

Late Colonel Peerdan and many Colonels, Majors, IPS officers make the village proud.

Agriculture

Farming, dairy, vegetable cultivation and businesses sustain livelihoods.

Our Vision

To preserve heritage while advancing education, unity and sustainable development.
